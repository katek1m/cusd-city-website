Recruitment Information

Recruitment for CUSD takes place in the beginning of the Fall and Spring Semester. Fill out the application form here and select Sustainable Mobility: Garage/City.

Majors of interest:

  • Urban and Regional Studies
  • Design and Environmental Analysis (DEA)
  • Policy Analysis and Management (PAM)
  • Information Science

But students from any major with an interest can join!
