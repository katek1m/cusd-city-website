Recruitment Information

Recruitment for CUSD takes place in the beginning of the Fall and Spring Semester. Find the application form here and select Sustainable Mobility: Garage/City.

Majors of interest:

  • Urban and Regional Studies
  • Design and Environmental Analysis (DEA)
  • Policy Analysis and Management (PAM)
  • Information Science

But students from any major with an interest can join!

Hear from our former lead on why you should join!

People should join because it's an opportunity to examine real-life complex problems and try to find real solutions! The experience is unlike anything else you can find on campus and it brings meaning to your life in a way that academic coursework can't.

Sabrina Martin — Information Science '23
