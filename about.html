Who We Are

Sustainable Mobility: City (previously Garage) is a subteam under Cornell University Sustainable Design (CUSD), an interdisciplinary project team with over 250 undergraduate and graduate students from all seven colleges working together to achieve ecological, social, and economic sustainability through providing innovative solutions.
