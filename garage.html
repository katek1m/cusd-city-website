Seneca Street Parking Garage Signage Implementation

Background

In Spring 2019, the team began a project aimed at applying systems engineering and design thinking to improve the Seneca Garage signage and user systems. The team seeks to build a portal to downtown Ithaca by creating a comfortable, safe, and efficient user experience that fosters an eagerness to further explore the city.

Problem brief

The current signage is cluttered, visually inconsistent, and difficult to read. Signs and instructions were piled on each other with no cohesive system in place.

The objective

  • Improve the garage signage and user systems while enhancing the overall look and feel of the garage by incorporating elements of local nature and Ithaca itself into an otherwise purely functional space.
  • Build a portal to downtown Ithaca by creating a comfortable, safe, and efficient user experience that fosters an eagerness to further explore the city.
