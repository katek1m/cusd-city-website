Passionate about
local impact

We believe in making a difference in our local community.

Cornell students have an abundance of resources and skills, but treat their time in Ithaca as transient. Our team finds it very fulfilling to contribute to and participate in the community that hosts us during our formative college years. Sometimes the most meaningful challenges to work on are in your own "backyard" and this team is the epitome of that sentiment!

Our Mission

Our team is focused on brining a renewed sense of identity, community, and ownership to often overlooked areas in the city of Ithaca. We take an interdisciplinary approach involving social sciences, urban planning, systems engineering, and design thinking.
